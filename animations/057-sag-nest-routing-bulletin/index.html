Idea

Turn the recent Sag & Nest foldout image lane into a living browser object: a neighborhood map with route slips, bench stops, and soft residents that feels printable and navigable at the same time.

Interaction

Drag cards, stir the field with the pointer, and switch the shared weather mode. The response stays embedded in the map through route particles, slow clustering, and paper shifts rather than fake app chrome.
