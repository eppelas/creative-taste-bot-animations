Motion logic

Pointer movement does not push particles around. It behaves like a rack-focus bias, quietly weighting one layer over another. Click injects a diagonal shutter sweep that leaves a short afterimage like passing scooter light on wet glass.

Branch correction

This answers the repeated request for more editorial, cinematic, and human scenes with reflections, focus changes, and long-exposure traces, instead of more detached infographic cards or generalized creature noise.

Frame discipline

The readouts stay outside the scene. Inside the picture there are only glass decals, receipt strips, rain marks, and urban glow, so the page still feels like an image being made rather than a tool being demonstrated.
