Idea

Interaction

Hover changes salt brightness, wind angle, droplet density, and terrace warmth only near the cursor while click rotates the whole atlas through three climate laws.

Next

Branch break

Scale shift over repetition

Formal law

Material cue

Salt, glass, paper, mist
