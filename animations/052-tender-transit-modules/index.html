Code Animation Study #052

Tender Transit Modules

A browser-native editorial sheet that answers the recent branch correction by returning to humans instead of another creature or control dossier: several small public-body scenes breathe on one page while rain reflections, route lines, and handwritten notes keep the surface alive.

Move across the sheet to bring nearby people into focus and drag their reflection bands sideways. Click anywhere to send a short commute pulse through the notes and figure links.

Human editorial lane Rain-focus interaction Static self-contained page

Idea

Build a design-usable sheet of people fragments and transit moments, so the animation library stops collapsing every branch into strange organisms or glossy interfaces.

Interaction

Pointer proximity works like a moving lens: figures sharpen, labels lean, and watery route reflections shear locally instead of exposing a separate control panel.

Next

This could grow into a larger family of human module pages: editorial street scenes, awkward tender gestures, or more realistic portrait fragments with stronger environmental logic.
