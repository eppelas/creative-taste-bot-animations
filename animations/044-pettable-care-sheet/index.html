Pettable Care Sheet

A flatter illustrated care poster where several awkward rooted beings share one page, pass comfort between each other, and answer touch directly instead of hiding behind another dashboard or folio.

Move close to a body to tilt its gaze, wake its roots, and brush the connecting thread. Press or drag across the page to pet a creature: the nearest one blushes, leans, and sends a brief warmth pulse across the whole small colony.
