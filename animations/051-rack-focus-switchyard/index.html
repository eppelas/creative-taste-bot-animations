Idea

Multipane cinematic control surface

This study shifts away from creatures into a page that feels closer to a design-usable interface poster: offset windows, focus-rack echoes, calibration guides, and live signal traffic with no single central emblem.

Interaction

Repel, retune, plant anchors

Move the pointer to repel new branch growth from the cursor and intensify the nearest pane. Click to plant up to six temporary anchors that keep bending traffic long after the pointer leaves.
