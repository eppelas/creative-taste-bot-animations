Code Animation Study #054

Hushed Figure Switchsheet

A browser-native human illustration page that picks up the strongest recent reusable lane without repeating the dark control-surface loop. Small awkward figures live on one paper sheet while shared route lines, weather bands, and focus pulses keep the layout breathing.

Move across the page to sharpen the nearest vignette and tug its weather sideways. Click to cycle the sheet mood between queue, hush, and drift without turning the page into fake app chrome.

Idea

Keep the design-usable human lane alive as modular illustrated fragments, so the library does not collapse every fresh request into either a creature dossier or a heavy interface dashboard.

Interaction

Pointer proximity behaves like a quiet lens: nearby figures sharpen, their route bands slide, and the shared paper weather bends around the active card instead of adding explicit controls over the art.

Next

A later variant could let the sheet accept short text labels or user-uploaded note snippets, so the figure modules reorganize around real editorial fragments instead of a fixed idle loop.
